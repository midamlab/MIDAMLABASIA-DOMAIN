Triple Action Mechanism

① LYSIS — Bromelain

Targets and melts unnecessary proteins while rapidly reducing localized swelling and puffiness for immediate facial contouring.

② TRANSPORT — Lecithin

Acts as a powerful emulsifier, breaking down waste structures and enhancing deep dermal delivery of all active ingredients.

③ STABILIZE — Centella & Peptides

Immediately rebuilds the barrier, ensuring zero irritation and leaving a serene, taut, and refined finish.

Sculpted Contour

Professional Clinic Protocol

  • Preparation — Cleanse the face thoroughly. Distribute the D.D. CUT ampoule evenly onto the target areas (jawline, cheeks, submental region).
  • Device Integration (Synergy) — Combine with LDM (Ultrasound) or Electroporation (EP) devices. The micro-massage enhances lymphatic drainage while maximizing deep dermal absorption of proteins.
  • Seal & Finish — Finish with a cooling modeling mask or RED LED therapy to lock in active ingredients and accelerate the Centella calming effect.

Treatment Protocol

  • Advanced Consultation — Detailed skin assessment required. Discontinue retinoids and active exfoliants 7 days prior.
  • Pre-Treatment — Double-cleanse, stabilize pH at 5.0–5.5. Topical anesthetic recommended for sensitive skin.
  • Application — Apply per Enhanced MPPT™ protocol. Monitor skin response and adjust contact time per tolerance.
  • Post-Treatment — Neutralize, apply recovery mask 20 min. Prescribe gentle cleanser, HA serum, and SPF 50+ for 5 days.
  • Course & Interval — 3–4 sessions at 3–4 week intervals. Peak results at 3–6 months post-final session.
