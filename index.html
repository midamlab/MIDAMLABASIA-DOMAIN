All-in-One Skin Remodeling · MPPT™ Technology

ViVi — Next-Gen All-in-One Skin Remodeling

ViVi offers a premium ampoule solution that seamlessly combines gentle peeling with effective collagen stimulation, ensuring that clients experience the best skin transformations without downtime or discomfort. Designed specifically for professional skincare clinics, ViVi targets the need for results-driven treatments that provide visible benefits — all while ensuring a gentle and non-invasive experience.

MPPT™ Technology — 4-Step Mechanism

1

OPEN — BHA (Pathway Activation)

Beta Hydroxy Acid clarifies pores and opens absorption pathways for high-performance results. Its lipophilic nature allows deep penetration into lipid-rich pores to dissolve sebum.

2

REFINE — PHA (Hydra-Resurfacing)

Polyhydroxy Acid smooths skin texture while activating deep hydration for a luminous glow. With 8 hydroxyl groups, it acts as a "moisture magnet" with high efficacy and minimal irritation.

3

SIGNAL — PLLA (Volume Stimulation)

PLLA and peptides send signals to boost natural collagen, restoring youthful density and firmness. Biodegradable microparticles trigger sustained neocollagenesis for long-term structural volume.

4

RENEW — HA (Total Rejuvenation)

Continuous renewal cycles result in balanced, healthy, and visibly rejuvenated skin. High-molecular-weight HA binds and retains water up to 1,000 times its own weight.

Key Active Ingredients

PLLA

PLLA — Structural Volumizing & Dermal Plumping

A biodegradable polymer composed of repeating L-lactic acid units. As these particles slowly biodegrade within the skin, they trigger sustained neocollagenesis, inducing natural collagen production for long-term structural volume.

BHA

BHA — Precision Exfoliation & Path Opening

An aromatic compound with adjacent hydroxyl and carboxyl groups on a benzene ring. Its lipophilic (oil-soluble) nature allows it to penetrate deeply into lipid-rich pores to dissolve sebum and clear pathways for deeper ingredient absorption.

PHA

PHA — Intensive Hydration & Radiance Activation

A complex acid formed by the combination of gluconic acid and galactose. With 8 hydroxyl groups, it acts as a "moisture magnet." Its large molecular size ensures gradual skin absorption, providing high efficacy with minimal irritation.

HA

HA — Structural Volumizing & Dermal Plumping

A high-molecular-weight polysaccharide consisting of alternating D-glucuronic acid and N-acetylglucosamine. Its long-chain structure has the unique ability to bind and retain water molecules up to 1,000 times its own weight.

Treatment Protocol

  • Advanced Consultation — Mandatory detailed skin assessment for all first-time High ViVi clients. Review sensitivity threshold, previous peel history, retinoid use, and any active inflammatory conditions. Discontinue all retinoids and active exfoliants 7 days prior.
  • Pre-Treatment Preparation — Double-cleanse and degrease skin thoroughly. Apply pre-treatment barrier primer to stabilize pH at 5.0–5.5. Topical anesthetic (30 min) recommended for sensitive skin types due to higher acid concentration.
  • High ViVi Application — Apply in controlled phases per the Enhanced MPPT™ protocol. Practitioner monitors skin response at each phase. Controlled flushing and mild tingling are expected. Adjust contact time per client tolerance.
  • Post-Treatment Management — Neutralize as directed. Apply medical-grade recovery mask with Centella Asiatica and HA for 20 minutes. Prescribe home care: gentle cleanser, HA serum, and SPF 50+ strict sun avoidance for 5 days.
  • Course & Interval — Recommended course: 3–4 sessions at 3–4 week intervals (longer spacing than ViVi due to higher potency). Maximum results typically observed 3–6 months post-final session. Annual maintenance recommended.
