Treatment Protocol

  • Advanced Consultation — Detailed skin assessment required. Discontinue retinoids and active exfoliants 7 days prior.
  • Pre-Treatment — Double-cleanse, stabilize pH at 5.0–5.5. Topical anesthetic recommended for sensitive skin.
  • Application — Apply per Enhanced MPPT™ protocol. Monitor skin response and adjust contact time per tolerance.
  • Post-Treatment — Neutralize, apply recovery mask 20 min. Prescribe gentle cleanser, HA serum, and SPF 50+ for 5 days.
  • Course & Interval — 3–4 sessions at 3–4 week intervals. Peak results at 3–6 months post-final session.
